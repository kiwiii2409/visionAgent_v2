# Thunderbird Email Client

## Composing and Sending an Email

1. Click the **"Write"** or **"New Message"** button in the toolbar to open a compose window
2. In the compose window, fill in:
   - **To:** — recipient email address (click the To field first, then type)
   - **Subject:** — press Tab after To field, then type subject, make a subject if not mentioned
   - **Body:** — press Tab to reach the main editing area, then type message content
3. Click the **"Send"** button (usually in the top-left toolbar) to send the email
4. Click the **"Sent"** button to check whether the mail is sent

## Important Notes

- Do NOT edit contacts in the Address Book when you need to send an email — that opens a contact editor, not an email compose window
- The Write/New Message button opens a fresh compose window where you can enter recipient, subject, and body
- If you see "Contact" or "Address Book", you are in the wrong place — look for the Write/New Message button
- When the email is sent successfully, Thunderbird may briefly show a "Sending message" dialog, then the compose window closes
